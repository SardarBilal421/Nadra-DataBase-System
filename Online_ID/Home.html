Availability of Fingerprints for Services in Pakistan

If you intend to use any service(SIM issuance, opening bank account, vehicle registration etc) in Pakistan that requires fingerprint verification, your fingerprints must be on file with NADRA. To determine whether your fingerprints can be used for these services, please use the form.



Availability of Processing Facility

  • For ease of applicants residing in rural areas of Pakistan, online processing facility is also available at selected Pakistan Post Offices – Locations

  • Online Processing facility is also available at 8 x International Airports of Pakistan at the departure lounge – Locations

General Guidelines to Apply Online

  1. Register an account

  2. Choose relevant category and start your application

  3. Fill in application form online

  4. Pay fee through Debit/ Credit card (Payment Guidelines)

  5. Scan and upload photograph (Photograph Guidelines)

  6. Scan and upload fingerprint form (Fingerprint Guidelines)

  7. Upload required documents

  8. Submit the application

  9. It is advised that for attachment of all supporting documents (i.e. passport, ID cards etc), color scanned image may be attached with at least 200 dpi.
